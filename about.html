About the Coaches

Camp Director Kamran Yashouafar

Kamran has played club soccer for Beverly Hills United,
started for the Milken varsity team, and competed in AYSO.
He has also privately trained kids for three years and runs
a soccer camp each summer, helping young athletes
develop their skills and love for the game.

Camp Pro Coach Ariel Malekan

Ariel has played for AYSO United and was captain of
the JV soccer team for Beverly Hills High School.
He coaches B-12 All Stars for AYSO for the past
couple of months. He started coaching and training
younger age groups despite his ACL injury.
