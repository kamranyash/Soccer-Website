About the Coaches

Camp Director Kamran Yashouafar

Kamran has competed at various levels of youth soccer, including
club play with Beverly Hills United, varsity competition at
Milken, and participation in AYSO. In addition to his playing
experience, he has spent the past three years privately
training young athletes and leads a summer soccer camp, where
he helps players build both their skills and passion for the game.

Camp Pro Coach Ariel Malekan

Ariel has played for AYSO United and served as captain
of the junior varsity soccer team at Beverly Hills High
School. Despite recovering from an ACL injury, he began
coaching and training younger age groups and currently
coaches the Boys U12 All-Star team for AYSO, demonstrating
leadership and dedication to the sport.
