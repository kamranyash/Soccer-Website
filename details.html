Details About Camp

What a Day in Camp is Composed Of

Morning Warm-up & Drills
Light stretching and dynamic warm-ups
Dribbling, passing, and shooting drills
Small-group skill-building exercises

Scrimmage Time
Divide into teams for a friendly game
Focus on teamwork, positioning, and communication

Conditioning & Running
Speed and agility drills Endurance-building exercises
Fun relay races and challenges

Fun Games with the Kids
Soccer-based mini-games (e.g., knockout, crossbar challenge)
Other engaging activities to build confidence and enjoyment

Daily Park Visit
Outdoor break at a local park
Free play, snacks, and relaxation before the next session

Important Details

Age Group: 7-12 year olds.

Camp Hours: 9am-2pm

Price: $250 per child, per week.

Location: Westwood Recreation- Tommy's Field

Various other details will be disclosed in emails prior to Camp.
